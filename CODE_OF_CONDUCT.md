# Code of Conduct

Sovereign Conclave is a decision-support project. Disagreement is expected; bad
faith behavior is not.

## Expected Behavior

- Argue technical and design claims with evidence.
- Keep criticism specific, actionable, and tied to the work.
- Preserve dissent without personal attacks.
- Respect privacy and do not pressure contributors to disclose sensitive
  evidence, credentials, affiliations, or private deployment details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory conduct.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Submitting secrets, credentials, or confidential artifacts to the repository.
- Using persona names to launder unsupported authority or harmful claims.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer decisions about scope, safety, or licensing.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block
contributors whose behavior damages the project or its users.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact a maintainer privately when
possible. Include links, dates, and the minimum context needed to evaluate the
issue.
